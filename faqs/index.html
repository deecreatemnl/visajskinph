Customer FAQ

What skin types are viSaj Skin products suitable for?+

There is something for everyone! Our Matcha bath line is suitable for acne-ridden or sensitive skin, while our Le Blanc serum and soap is for those looking to brighten their skin with gentle actives. Full ingredient lists are available on each product page and for any concerns, we recommend doing a patch test.

Are your products vegan and do you test on animals?+

You can be rest assured that all viSaj Skin products are vegan and tested on real people, even before production started. Our products went through a few trials and errors before being available on the market.

Are viSaj Skin products suitable for pregnant or lactating moms?+

Though nothing in our formula has been identified as unsafe for pregnant or breastfeeding persons, we recommend sharing our ingredients list with your healthcare providers for review before using.

What is the expiry date of your products?+

The shelf life of our products is two years when unopened and stored at room temperature. Upon opening, please keep products stored away from direct sunlight and at a room temperature.

Are viSaj Skin products clean?+

viSaj Skin products are formulated with high global standards and gentle ingredients. Though we are direct-to-consumer, our ingredients lists are in line with the clean standards specified by our manufacturers and are FDA approved.
