Our Story

Skincare with Purpose

Born in the Philippines, made for the Filipino skin — viSaj believes clean ingredients and beautiful skin can co-exist.

Who We Are

Rooted in Filipino Values

viSaj Skin was founded with a simple idea: Filipinos deserve skincare that actually works for our climate, our skin, and our planet. We formulate with ingredients that are trending in Asia's biggest skincare markets — but adapted for the everyday Filipino.

Our products are made with ceremonial-grade matcha, niacinamide, kojic acid, snail mucin, and glycolic acid — ingredients with proven track records. No harsh sulfates, no unnecessary chemicals.

Every product is vegan, cruelty-free, and made with the Earth in mind.

Our Ingredients

The Power of Matcha & Actives

Ceremonial-grade matcha is rich in antioxidants, anti-inflammatory compounds, and chlorophyll — making it a powerful skincare ingredient that protects against free radicals, reduces excess sebum, and promotes an even skin tone.

Our Le Blanc line harnesses kojic acid, glycolic acid, niacinamide, and snail mucin to gently brighten, exfoliate, and hydrate — delivering a visible glow from the very first use.
