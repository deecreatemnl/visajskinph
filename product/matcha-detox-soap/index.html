Matcha Detox Soap

Known as an antioxidant and anti-inflammatory in a bar, our matcha soap is proven to reduce excess sebum in the body to promote acne-free skin all over.

viSaj’s Matcha bath line boasts high-grade ceremonial matcha as the main ingredient, which is an antioxidant that helps protect your skin against free radicals. Matcha slows down cellular aging and helps promote even skin tone, all around. It is a trending ingredient in Japan for this reason.

viSaj’s products are vegan, not tested on animals and are mindful to the Earth.

Key Benefits

Reduces excess sebum, cleans skin without stripping moisture

Who It's For

Sensitive, acne prone or dry skin.

How to Use

Lather generously onto damp skin, avoiding the eye area, before rinsing the body thoroughly. Use daily with our matcha scrub for best results.

Why It's Different

This unique bar soap is packed with antioxidants which helps maintain and protect your skin barrier.Its also made with clean, eco-friendly ingredients from the Philippines.
